I was born in Charleston, SC in 1985. My family owned a large plot of land right on a river just outside of town. I think thats why I developed a love for the water.

As a youngster, all I really cared about was sports baseball andbasketball. Later I picked up golf and played that until I injured my wrist and was forced to quit.

After failing my way out of college in my early and mid twenties, my life began again when I joined the Navy. These four years would be some of the most formative of my entire life. They set the stage for me to learn that I loved to travel and experience other cultures around the world.

Being a veteran would open many doors for me professionally. I would go on to be recruited by Rockwell Automation for a program exclusively for veterans to train and replace an aging work force in the manufacturing industry. This is where I learned programming fundamentals that ultimately led me to where I am today, enrolled in a full stack web development immersive program and hopefully leading toward starting my own company...
